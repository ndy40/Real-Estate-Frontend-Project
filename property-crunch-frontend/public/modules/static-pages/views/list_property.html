FAQ

Yes. You can add additional users to your account. Creating a new user is easy, log into your account and follow the instructions.

Yes you can. You can cancel your package at any time. Log into your account and you can find the cancel button under your account settings.

What happens at the end of the trial period?

Your listings will stay active for a few more days. Our team will get in touch to discuss how to upgrade your account.

Is there any set up cost?

No. There is no set-up cost or hidden charges.

Is there a cost or charge for listing?

No. There is no cost involved. You can start uploading properties today for FREE.

How long before new property listings are live on the website?

Almost immediately. However, allow a few hours for the listing to appear on the website. We might get in touch with you if there are any issues with the property you uploaded.
